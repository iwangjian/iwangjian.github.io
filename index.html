Research

My research interests are in natural language processing (NLP), large language models (LLMs), and AI for Science more broadly. Recently, I have been focusing on the following directions:

  • LLM-based Agents: world models for agents (arXiv'26), embodied agents (ACL Findings'26a), task tutoring (ACL Findings'25a), agentic RL (arXiv'25a), context/memory management, etc.
  • Reasoning and Interaction: strategic reasoning (ACL'26a), situated interaction (ACL'26b), reasoning belief (ACL Findings'26b) test-time scaling (arXiv'25b, AAAI'26), latent reasoning (arXiv'25c), multi-turn consistency (ACL'24), etc.
  • AI for Biomedicine: AI agents for drug discovery and clinical healthcare.
