Research

My research interests are in natural language processing (NLP), large language models (LLMs), and AI more broadly. Recently, I have been focusing on the following directions:

  • Reasoning and Reliability of LLMs: test-time scaling (arXiv'25a, arXiv'25b), safety alignment (ACL'25), latent reasoning (arXiv'25c), etc.
  • Digital and Embodied Agents: embodied reasoning, environment adaptation, agentic RL (arXiv'25), tutoring agents (ACL-Findings'25a), task planning(ACL-Findings'25b), etc.
  • Conversational AI: proactive dialogue systems (TNNLS'23, TOIS'24, ACL-Findings'23), dialogue consistentcy (ACL'24), personalized role-playing (EMNLP'23), emotional support conversation (AAAI'24), etc.
